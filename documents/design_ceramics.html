unipodal bridge

steps
  • lie down on the floor facing upwards.
  • bend one leg bringing the foot to the knee.
  • rapidly push up with your bent leg squeezing the buttocks.
  • slowly lower back down and repeat by switching to the other leg.

    • thruster with rubber bands

    steps
  • place rubber band under your feet and squat down.
  • grab the bands with both hands and rapidly stand up stretching your arms upwards.
  • slowly squat down and repeat.
    • advice
  • make sure your legs and feet line up with your shoulders laterally and keep them straight forward.
